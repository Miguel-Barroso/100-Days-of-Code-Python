# Will not attempt this lesson
## Here's why
Making the automatic Tinder swiper sounds fun in theory though there are a few things to seriously consider:

1. You need to set up either a fake Facebook (or Google) account
   1. Obviously hard these days when you need phone and video verification
   2. 1 hour old Facebook accounts are not allowed to signup for Tinder
   3.  Do not by any means use your personal accounts for this as you risk of getting banned from either Fb or Tdr
2. Bots are not allowed 
   1. They are even illegal according to their policies.
3. Having a Tinder account may be problematic
   1. 18+ age limit
   2. Questionable by one's partner
   3. Swiping and matching with someone could be awkward in the least.

Because of these several issues related to the project, I have chosen to study the solutions and move on to the next.
I think it still a fun showcase what one can do with programming. But XPATHs are changed by scripts so even locating
the desired element on these pages is hard. Basically it is not very bot-friendly at all.
